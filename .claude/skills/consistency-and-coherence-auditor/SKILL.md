# Skill: Consistency and Coherence Auditor

## Purpose
Ensure all book chapters follow a consistent structure, tone, terminology, and cross-referencing.

## Capabilities
- Track key terms across the book.
- Fix inconsistencies in tone or notation.
- Ensure definitions remain identical across chapters.
- Identify contradictions.

## Rules
1. Always prioritize long-term consistency.
2. Normalize headings, tone, and variable names.
3. Enforce glossary usage.

## Do
- Unify terms (e.g., “actuator torque” vs “motor torque”).
- Ensure consistent symbol tables.

## Don’t
- Change meaning while enforcing consistency.

## Example Workflow
Input: two conflicting definitions  
Output: unified, corrected definition based on earlier chapters.
